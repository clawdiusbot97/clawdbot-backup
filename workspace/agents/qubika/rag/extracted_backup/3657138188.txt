Introduction
With the new setup of our corporate WARP VPN, we can now access Bancard servers more securely and efficiently. This document explains how to configure and use the VPN, along with the necessary IP mapping to connect to Bancard servers from our internal network.
Configuring the WARP VPN
If you already have the WARP VPN installed and configured, you'll need to log out and log back in for the new configuration to take effect. Follow the steps below to log out:
Logging Out of WARP:
Click the WARP icon in the top-right menu bar.

Click the settings icon.

Select "Preferences."

Go to the "Account" tab.

Click the "Logout from Cloudflare Zero Trust" button.

After logging out, follow the instructions in the attached document to log back into the WARP VPN.
Note:If you haven't configured the VPN yet, follow the instructions in the attached document for the initial setup.
Documento de Configuración:

Using the VPN
Once the VPN is configured, activating it is simple:
Click the WARP icon in the top-right menu bar.

Toggle the switch to activate the VPN.

That’s it! You are now connected to the WARP VPN.
