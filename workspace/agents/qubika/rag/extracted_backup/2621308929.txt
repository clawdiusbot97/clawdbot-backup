Guide for logging into Microsoft Teams

Download Microsoft Teams
If you haven't already, download and install the Microsoft Teams application on your device.
https://www.microsoft.com/en/microsoft-teams/download-app

Wait to receive access from Bancard via email
You need to wait to receive an email from the Bancard Team containing the necessary login details for Microsoft Teams.

Sign in to Teams with your Moove-IT email
Open the Microsoft Teams application on your device and select the "Sign in" option. Enter your Moove-IT email address and click "Next" or the arrow icon.

Choose "Sign-in options" > "Sign in to an organization"
Once you've entered your email address, you'll be presented with a screen to select sign-in options. Choose "Sign-in options" and then select "Sign in to an organization".

Paste the organization’s domain name
In the provided field, paste the organization domain name that was sent to you via email from Bancard. Make sure to copy and paste the name accurately.

Paste the verification code
After entering the organization name, you'll receive an additional email with a verification code. Open your email client, find the verification email, and copy the code.

Enter the verification code
Return to the Microsoft Teams sign-in screen and paste the verification code into the corresponding field. Ensure that the code is entered correctly.

Complete the sign-in process
Once you've entered the verification code, click the "Sign in" or "Accept" button to complete the sign-in process. If the verification code is correct, you'll be taken to your Microsoft Teams account associated with the specified organization.
