## Hallazgos

### 1) Document AI + RAG for Insurance (Production-Ready)
**What:** AI-powered document extraction for insurance PDFs now integrates RAG for governance and achieves 99%+ accuracy on noisy uploads. Vendors like Inaza, AZAPI.ai, and GroupBWT offer end-to-end workflows: ingest → OCR/NLP → classify → extract with confidence scores → validate → route.

**Why it matters for Brokia:** Policy details, claims info, and risk factors can be auto-extracted from PDFs/scans with full traceability. This enables straight-through processing at scale (30→2,000+ users).

**Links:**
- https://www.inaza.com/apis/ai-data-extraction-for-insurance-documents
- https://azapi.ai/blog/best-motor-insurance-claim-document-extraction-api-in-2026/
- https://groupbwt.com/blog/data-extraction-insurance/

**Actionable idea:** Pilot AZAPI.ai or Inaza for motor/auto claims extraction; test on RC books, FIRs, repair estimates. Measure straight-through processing rate lift vs manual.

---

### 2) Voice AI Agents for WhatsApp/Telegram (Low-Latency Deployed)
**What:** respond.io, Smallest.ai, Seasalt.ai, and AnveVoice offer real-time voice AI TTS agents integrated with WhatsApp/Telegram. Features: sub-second latency, 32-language auto-detection, multi-turn dialogues, human handover, HIPAA/GDPR compliance.

**Why it matters for Brokia:** Uruguay has 95%+ WhatsApp penetration. Voice agents could handle 70-80% of customer inquiries (quotes, claims status, policy questions) 24/7 with natural conversation.

**Links:**
- https://respond.io/blog/whatsapp-ai-voice-agent
- https://smallest.ai/blog/whatsapp-beta-integrates-ai-suggested-topics-and-voice-chat
- https://anvevoice.app/integrations/telegram

**Actionable idea:** Deploy respond.io WhatsApp voice agent for quote inquiries. A/B test vs text chatbot; measure conversion rate and customer satisfaction (CSAT).

---

### 3) Deepfake Fraud Detection (Critical Threat)
**What:** Deepfake fraud surged 700% in Q1 2025. Insurance schemes include fake injury videos (4K AI-generated), synthetic identity "ghost policies," and voice deepfakes for social engineering. Solutions: identifAI (media forensics), Experian Ascend Platform (avoided $19B losses in 2025), F3 Investigations (GAN detectors + metadata forensics).

**Why it matters for Brokia:** Fraudulent claims directly impact loss ratios. As a broker, verifying claim authenticity before carrier submission is a value-add (and risk reduction).

**Links:**
- https://identifai.net/identifai-deepfake-detection-for-the-insurance/
- https://www.experianplc.com/newsroom/press-releases/2026/experian-s-new-fraud-forecast-warns-agentic-ai--deepfake-job-can
- https://f3investigations.com/insurance-fraud-in-2026-emerging-schemes-and-how-to-spot-them/

**Actionable idea:** Integrate identifAI or similar into claims intake workflow. Flag suspicious media (photos/videos) for secondary review before carrier submission.

### 4) Data Residency & Private AI (Compliance Requirement)
**What:** EU AI Act requires documented data governance by Aug 2026 (7% global turnover penalty). India RBI/SEBI mandates in-country storage. China PIPL is strictest. Solution: on-prem/VPC AI deployments keep processing within sovereign infrastructure. PremAI offers fine-tuning with cryptographic verification.

**Why it matters for Brokia:** Uruguay insurance data may face cross-border restrictions. Private deployment ensures compliance and builds trust with local carriers/regulators.

**Links:**
- https://blog.premai.io/ai-data-residency-requirements-by-region-the-complete-enterprise-compliance-guide/
- https://secureprivacy.ai/blog/ai-risk-compliance-2026

**Actionable idea:** Architect Brokia AI stack with data residency in mind. Evaluate sovereign cloud providers (not US hyperscalers) for Uruguay/LATAM deployment.

### 5) Broker Copilots + Agentic AI (Claims Automation)
**What:** "Copilots" are AI assistants for broker workflows (CRM, placements, pricing, portfolio). Agentic AI (autonomous agents) will resolve 80% of claims issues by 2029. Send.technology and Vertafore report broker adoption accelerating.

**Why it matters for Brokia:** A broker copilot differentiates vs traditional brokers. Automate submissions, carrier comparison, and client communication.

**Links:**
- https://send.technology/resources/blog/top-10-insurance-industry-trends-shaping-underwriting-in-2026/
- https://www.vertafore.com/resources/blog/2026-insurance-agency-trends
- https://britenet.eu/blog-post/insurance-trends-for-2026-how-artificial-intelligence-will-shape-the-industry/

**Actionable idea:** Build MVP broker copilot for auto insurance submissions: ingest client data → query multiple carriers → present comparison → generate proposal. Start with 2-3 carrier APIs.

### 6) Continuous Underwriting via IoT
**What:** "Continuous underwriting" uses IoT streaming data (telematics, sensors) for dynamic pricing and real-time risk adjustment. Claims reductions of 30-50% reported.

**Why it matters for Brokia:** Differentiate by offering usage-based insurance (UBI) with real-time pricing. Partner with telematics providers.

**Links:**
- https://www.indiainsurtech.com/pioneering-connectivity-six-insurtech-trends-uniting-the-industry-in-2026

**Actionable idea:** Explore partnership with telematics provider (e.g., Geotab, Samsara) for commercial fleet UBI product. Validate demand with 5-10 prospect fleets.
