# 10 — Stage Applicability Remap (ANTE vs FINAL vs DEFENSE vs LEGAL/CONTRACT)

Goal: make explicit which requirements apply to **Anteproyecto**, which apply only to **Final Delivery**, which apply to **Defense**, and which are **Legal/Contract** only.

Stages used:
- **Anteproyecto**
- **Final Delivery** (includes *format-only* requirements that matter at final submission)
- **Defense**
- **Legal/Contract**

> Note: many “format” requirements are enforced in the *final document* and/or after defense corrections; they are intentionally **not** treated as anteproyecto requirements.

## Notes / edge cases

- Doc 302/303/306 items are treated as **Final Delivery** even when teams may draft parts earlier. The requirement is enforced at final submission + correction cycle.
- Some “process” rules from Doc 304 constrain the whole lifecycle; they are listed with multiple stages accordingly.
