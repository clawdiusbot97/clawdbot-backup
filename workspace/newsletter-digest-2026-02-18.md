## 📜 History

**History Facts: The skyscraper that spun 90° with people inside**
- In the 1930s, Indiana Bell HQ (11,000 tons, 8 stories) was rotated while 600 employees kept working
- Architect Kurt Vonnegut Sr. designed the feat—building moved 52 feet sideways, then pivoted over one month
- Ended within 1/16 inch of planned position; workers reportedly didn't feel the movement

*Why it matters:* Remarkable industrial-age engineering story with cultural connection to Vonnegut.
