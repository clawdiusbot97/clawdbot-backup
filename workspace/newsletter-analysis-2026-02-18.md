**Subject:** They spun a skyscraper… with people inside
**Sender:** hello@historyfacts.com
**Date:** Feb 18, 2026

**Summary:**
- In the 1930s, the Indiana Bell Telephone Company headquarters (11,000 tons, 8 stories) was rotated 90 degrees while 600 employees continued working inside
- Architect Kurt Vonnegut Sr. (father of the author) designed the engineering feat
- Building moved 52 feet sideways at 15 inches/hour, then pivoted 90 degrees over one month
